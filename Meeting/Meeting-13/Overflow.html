Overflow-x and Overflow-y

You Can Also Change Content Overflow Horizontally Or Vertically.

Overflow-x Specifies what to do with the left/right side of the content.

Overflow-y determines what to do with the top/bottom of the content.

You can use the overflow property when you want more control over the layout. The overflow property determines what happens if content overflows an element's content.
